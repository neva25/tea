LAW India (Looms & Weaves) is a woman owned social enterprise, started in 2013, with a goal of ensuring safe, natural food free of pesticides and chemicals to create a healthier society. It organizes organic and fair trade farmers from different parts of the country, especially women famers, with a vision to produce an environmentally and socially responsible, ethical business of organic and handcrafted products of excellence. In this way, it generates employment to the down-trodden, making them economically independent. It promotes sustainable agriculture by promoting permaculture-thereby not harming the eco-system-and also shares a part of its profits to support the poor and marginalized. LAW offers world-class, ethically sourced, organic, natural foods and spices as well as handcrafted products.
