LAW India (looms & weaves) is a woman owned social enterprise, started in 2013. Our goal of ensuring safe food to the citizens of the country led us to start few initiatives for pesticide free and chemical free natural food. We then slowly moved to organize organic and fair trade farmers from different parts of the country, especially women famers. We envision a future where environmentally and socially responsible ethical business of organic and handcrafted products of excellence. To cater to the needs of the downtrodden by generating employment and adding value to the handcrafted wares created by rural women and the farming community To promote sustainable agriculture ground on permaculture To share a part of our profits to support the poor and marginalized people in the society. as well as for environmental sustainability. We offer world-class, ethically sourced, organic, natural and handcrafted products to the socially empowered customers. We put the health and wellbeing of our patrons or social investors as our first priority, striving to create a healthier India free from illnesses. We help small businesswomen earn a living, making them economically independent. LAW, India promotes a permaculture based small socio- ecological system
