In 1893 the James Finlay Group of Glasgow, Scotland, acquired the concession for tea cultivation in South India's Malabar Coast. In 1983, India's premier and leading business house, the Tata Group acquired the entire Finlay Group to re-christen it as Tata Tea Limited. Tata Tea Ltd. is one of India's leading tea companies and is the second largest supplier of branded tea in the world. producing 40 million kilograms tea on about three dozen plantations in India and Sri Lanka. A Company that believes that its success is defined by its customers and consumers and that their products should be top-level and add to the well-being of people the world over.
